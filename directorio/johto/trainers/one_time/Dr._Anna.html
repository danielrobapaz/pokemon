Dr. Anna

Dr. Anna (Japanese: アンナ Anna) is a character of the day who appeared in Hassle In The Castle. She also appeared in Ash & Pikachu.

In the anime

Dr. Anna runs a clinic, along with her assistant Keiko. Here, people with injuries are treated through the help of Pokémon. Among the many Pokémon at the clinic is her Zubat, named "Mimi", which she uses to X-ray her patients to find out what is wrong with them.

One day, Ash and his friends came along. Impressed with Mimi, Brock sent out his own Zubat. He asked Dr. Anna if she would be interested in letting him help out at the clinic, and she agreed.

After Team Rocket attacked, Brock sent out Zubat to stop them. During the battle, his Zubat evolved into Golbat. After it used Supersonic, Dr. Anna's machine—which she used to scan patients—broke. After Team Rocket was sent blasting off, Dr. Anna told Brock that she couldn't have him helping her out, since Golbat's Supersonic was far too powerful. Heartbroken once again, Brock continued on with Ash and Misty.

On hand

This listing is of Dr. Anna's known Pokémon:

Mimi ♀
Anna has a Zubat nicknamed Mimi (Japanese: ミミ Mimi) which she uses in her health clinic. Her Supersonic is used to examine the internal health of a patient. She can be distinguished from other Zubat by the green bow she wears.

She first appeared helping a patient with her Supersonic. Ash, Misty and Brock arrived at that moment, and after seeing Mimi, Brock sent out his own Zubat to help. Whilst Team Rocket were caught stealing food, Brock, Dr. Anna and both of the Zubat hunted them down, but fell down a hole into a strange maze.

To navigate through the maze, Anna and Brock ordered their Zubat to use Supersonic and generate a map of the maze. The Zubat eventually found the exit. But before they got out, Team Rocket nabbed both Zubat, but the huge explosion caused by Brock's Pineco freed them and they were reunited with their Trainers.

Mimi's only known move is Supersonic.

In the manga

Anna in the Ash & Pikachu manga

Ash & Pikachu

Dr. Anna appears in Treasure Hunting?! Try The Maze!, where she is simply known by "Anna" or "Miss Anna". Like in the anime, she works at a health clinic which uses Pokémon to help their patients. She is part of the tenth generation of her family to operate the Pokémon health clinic out of their castle.

Pokémon

On hand
Mimi
Like in the anime, Dr. Anna is shown with her Zubat, Mimi. When the group get lost in the maze hidden beneath the castle, she uses Mimi's Supersonic technique in conjunction with her computer to help navigate the maze and avoid traps.

Mimi's only known move is Supersonic.

Trivia

  • The name of her Zubat, Mimi, may have been derived from 耳 meaning ear on the basis that she uses her ears a lot.
