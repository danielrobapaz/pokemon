The park warden

The park warden is a character of the day who appeared in One Trick Phony!. He is in charge of the Battle Park.

He brought Team Rocket, who was working at the battle facility, more boxes of dirty Poké Balls for cleaning and laughed off their disdain, saying he had the hard task of shopping. He then left Team Rocket in charge of the park while he went to get supplies.

Later, when Team Rocket flew off with the Poké Balls and Ash's Pikachu, the warden ran out telling them that the Poké Balls belong to the Battle Park. After Ash's Totodile and Misty's Politoed brought down their balloon, Jessie and James sent out Venusaur, Charizard, and Blastoise to battle Ash and his friends. However, the rental Pokémon turned on them, as they were only obedient inside the park and blasted the trio off.

Various Pokémon
The Battle Park has a large variety of Pokémon, many of which were only featured on the display board in the entrance area. These Pokémon had already battled opponents and were not available for rent when Ash and his friends visited.

None of these Pokémon's moves are known.

Debut One Trick Phony!
Venusaur
Venusaur belonged to the Battle Park. It was borrowed by James when he battled Ash. Venusaur went up against Ash's Cyndaquil and won despite the type disadvantage. Its Vine Whip and Razor Leaf attacks were powerful enough to cut down trees.

Venusaur was sent out again to battle Ash and his friends, but it turned on Team Rocket and blasted them off.

Venusaur's known moves are Razor Leaf, Tackle, Vine Whip, and Solar Beam.

Debut One Trick Phony!
Voice actors
Japanese
English Eric Stuart
Charizard (×2)
Charizard was another Pokémon belonging to the Battle Park. The first Charizard appeared in the reception area alongside the other rental Pokémon, but it was unavailable because it still needed to recover. A second Charizard was borrowed by Jessie to battle Ash. Charizard went up against Ash's Totodile. It led much of the battle, though Totodile used Water Gun to reach Charizard's altitude and then knocked it out with another Water Gun.

Charizard was sent out again to battle Ash and his friends, but it turned on Team Rocket and sent them blasting off with Venusaur and Blastoise.

None of the first Charizard's moves are known. The second Charizard's known moves are Dig, Iron Tail, Scary Face, and Flamethrower.

Debut One Trick Phony!
Voice actors
Japanese Shin-ichiro Miki
English Shin-ichiro Miki
Blastoise
Blastoise was the third Pokémon belonging to the Battle Park. It was another Pokémon borrowed by Jessie to battle Ash. Blastoise went up against Ash's Pikachu and despite using some strong moves, such as Hydro Pump, that almost defeated Pikachu, Pikachu was able to counter Blastoise with Thunderbolt and Thunder. Jessie quickly recalled Blastoise before the match's result was officially called.

Blastoise was later sent out again to battle Ash and his friends, but it turned on Team Rocket and blasted them off with Hydro Pump.

Blastoise's known moves are Skull Bash, Bubble, Withdraw, and Hydro Pump.

Debut One Trick Phony!
